# ✨ Suggest People Feature - User Guide

## Overview
The "Suggest People" feature on your profile page shows personalized recommendations for travel buddies based on:
1. **Similar Travel Interests** - People who share your travel style, destination interests, and preferences
2. **Friends of Friends** - Connections of your friends who might be compatible matches

---

## How to Access

1. Go to your **Profile** page
2. Look for the **"Suggestions"** tab at the top
3. Click it to see personalized people recommendations

### Tab Navigation
```
Profile Page
├── Overview      ← Current profile info
├── Preferences   ← Your travel style & vibe scores
└── Suggestions   ← ✨ NEW: People recommendations
```

---

## What You'll See

### People with Similar Interests
Shows users matched by the **cosine similarity algorithm** - a smart matching system that considers:
- Travel style (Budget, Luxury, Adventure)
- Travel pace (Relaxed, Moderate, Fast-paced)
- Accommodation preference (Hostel, Hotel, Inn, Camping)
- Vibe scores (Budget-Luxury, Chill-Extreme, Solo-Group)
- Constraint tags (diet, lifestyle, values)
- Interests and destinations

**Similarity Score:** 0-100% match indicator
- 🟢 **75%+** = Excellent match (emerald)
- 🔵 **60-74%** = Good match (blue)
- 🟡 **45-59%** = Fair match (amber)
- ⚪ **<45%** = Show all matches (neutral)

### Friends of Friends
Displays people who are friends with your friends. Often discovers compatible travel buddies through existing connections.

---

## Card Information

Each suggested person shows:
- **Avatar** - Profile picture with fallback to initials
- **Name & Username** - Full name and @ handle
- **Location** - Where they're based
- **Bio** - Short description of their travel style
- **Similarity Score** - % match with you (color-coded)

## Important Notes

### ⚠️ KYC Requirement
The similarity matching algorithm requires **KYC verification**. If you haven't verified your identity:
1. Complete your **KYC** (Know Your Customer) verification
2. Wait for admin approval (usually 24-48 hours)
3. Once approved, similar users will populate automatically

### 🔄 Real-Time Updates
- Suggestions update when you:
  - Complete your KYC verification
  - Update your profile interests and preferences
  - Change your travel style or vibe scores
  - Add new constraint tags

### 📊 Algorithm Details
The matching system uses **cosine similarity** to compare:
- User interest vectors
- Travel preference profiles
- Constraint compatibility
- Age range compatibility

Result: Highly personalized, accurate recommendations

## Troubleshooting

### ❌ "No suggestions available yet"
**Causes:**
- You haven't completed KYC verification
- Your profile is incomplete
- No users match your criteria

**Solution:**
1. Check if KYC is approved
2. Complete your profile with interests and preferences
3. Wait 24-48 hours after KYC approval

### ❌ Similar users showing 0% match
**Causes:**
- Vastly different travel styles
- Incompatible constraint tags
- Different travel pace preferences

**Solution:**
- Check if your tags match the person
- Review their full profile for compatibility
- Consider "Friends of Friends" instead

### ❌ Can't send friend request
**Causes:**
- You're not KYC verified
- Request already exists
- They blocked you
- Their account is private

**Solution:**
- Complete KYC verification first
- Check if you already sent them a request
- Try adding through "View Profile" page

## Advanced Features

### Understanding Similarity Scores
```
Similarity Score = Cosine Similarity of vectors including:
├── Interest Tags (weighted by importance)
├── Travel Style (80-point scale)
├── Pace Preference 
├── Accommodation Type
├── Budget/Adventure/Social Levels
├── Age Range Compatibility
└── Constraint Tag Matching
```

### Constraint Compatibility
Some constraints are "soft" (affects score) while others are "hard" (blocks match):
- **Hard Blocks:** Strict vegetarian + non-vegetarian conflicts
- **Hard Blocks:** Non-smoker + smoker explicit preferences
- **Soft Factors:** Diet variations, lifestyle preferences

## FAQ

**Q: Why don't I see similar users?**
A: You likely need KYC approval. Check your profile for KYC status.

**Q: Can I unsend a friend request?**
A: Yes, go to their profile and click "Cancel Request"

**Q: How often do suggestions refresh?**
A: Automatically when you update your profile or KYC is approved.

**Q: Does similarity score change over time?**
A: Yes, if you or they update your profiles.

**Q: Can I see who viewed my profile?**
A: No, view history is private. Only friend requests are visible.

**Q: How accurate is the matching?**
A: Cosine similarity is proven accurate for recommendation systems. Your profile completeness affects accuracy.
